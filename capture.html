the real band project

a fully-functioning framework-free website for a british boy band that isn't one direction

background

capture the band capture (i’m a funny one!) in a one page website that i can hand off to them and run back to america and they can more or less cope with it.

challenge

count on/keep up with people who want lemony snicket-esque graphics and the next thing you know they want marty mcfly typefaces.

solution

an experimental promotional page leading to a simple and useful website almost completely made of twitter, songkick, and instagram feeds so the boys can keep playing, i can keep moving, and the site can keep current with little to no attention from us.
