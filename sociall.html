the promo project

an interface design to promote a rather clever app i haven't gotten round to making just yet.

background

sociall, an app to make connecting digitally with new friends natural, lacks strong branding and informative, friendly copy.

challenge

demonstrate creativity and develop a basic understanding of various visual design principles including layout and composition, colour and branding through the use of research, sketching and software.

solution

a bright and modern interface for a young and friendly crowd. i created a character for the brand who introduces the app through a scenario where it would have really come in handy for her. with this understanding, you are ushered into the site to be greeted by three of her many hands that guide you through the page dividing it into sections.

impact

i got a good feel for flat design and adobe illustrator and 45 dainty fingers later, realized the need for a mouse in my life.

module:

digital media design

role:

designer, first year, university of brighton
