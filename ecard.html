the make-uncle-doug-the-ecard-he's-always-dreamed-of project

background

my uncle doug has had a greeting card business for years and he had spoke to me before about turning it into an e-card business. as soon as i was given the christmas e-card brief, uncle doug’s e-card had it’s time to shine.

challenge

interpreting his greeting card into a full story with additional graphics.

solution

a twist on the original with the poor dog being taken out of his warm abode and into the original blistery snowstorm scene. i interpreted uncle doug’s illustrations into vector graphics and created a few other graphics that match the theme. i experimented with transitions, masks, and tweens to tell the story with classic e-card flare.

impact

a christmas card ready to send off to all our frenemies come december 2017 and a very smug uncle doug.

module:

interactive multimedia development

role:

graphic designer and animator, first year, university of brighton
