the dream band project

an interface design for a make-believe band that i'm a rockin' bassist in, but as it's not real i'll settle for rockin' website designer.

background

ok, i’ve gotta tell you somethin. the actual brief was to make a moody, grungy interface.

other thing - this works! i have taken all briefs at uni with lots of salt; hearing a snippet of the direction that i liked and would run with it to produce something i loved and people who don’t know me all that well can see me in my work and i think that is super cool!

challenge

work with the brief youthful, loud, energetic, moody, grunge, textured band interface without bursting into flames.

solution

impact

truly one of my favorite web designs yet. it’s clean. it’s nostalgic. it’s hip. it’s tropical.
